About Our School

East Land School was founded in 1995 with a vision to provide exceptional education that combines academic excellence with strong moral values. Located in Lagos, Nigeria, we are committed to nurturing well-rounded individuals who will contribute positively to our society.

Our mission is to empower students with knowledge, skills, and character needed to excel in an ever-changing world. We foster critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning in a supportive and inclusive environment.

At East Land School, we celebrate Nigeria's rich cultural heritage while preparing our students to be global citizens. Our values of integrity, respect, excellence, and community guide everything we do.

Our Programs

Primary Education

Our primary program provides a strong foundation in core subjects while nurturing curiosity and creativity. We focus on literacy, numeracy, and social skills development.

🎓

Secondary Education

Our secondary program prepares students for WAEC, NECO, and JAMB examinations while offering a broad curriculum that includes sciences, arts, and technology.

Admission Process

Join Our Community of Learners

We welcome applications from families who share our commitment to academic excellence and character development. Our admission process is designed to ensure the best fit for both students and the school.

Admission Requirements:

  • Completed application form
  • Previous school records (for transfer students)
  • Birth certificate or sworn declaration of age
  • Medical report from a licensed physician
  • Passport photographs

Applications are accepted year-round, but we recommend applying early as spaces are limited.
